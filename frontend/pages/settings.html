🛡️ Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect your name, email, interview session data, and performance metrics to provide personalized feedback and track progress.

Data Security

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Your resume text is stored securely and only used to generate interview questions.

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to power HireReady's features. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

AI Processing

Interview conversations are processed by the Claude AI API (Anthropic) to generate questions and evaluations. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting our support team.
